The Smart Rail Level Crossing project aims to enhance safety at railway crossings by implementing an automated system using ultrasonic sensors, servo motors, a buzzer, and an LCD display. The system detects approaching trains, alerts pedestrians and drivers, and closes the crossing gates to prevent accidents.
